Online World

At the start of the year we began by learning about the online world, we investigated things such as email protocols. For example, one of the protocols is called POP3 which allows users to read emails offline without an internet connection but only. if the email is downloaded to that device. Another protocol is SMTP (simple mail transfer protocol) this allows users to send messages to other users if there is an internet connection present.

Another topic we went over is Ubiquitous Computing which connects and integrates technology into everyday life. Such as your fridge, cameras, TVs and even your washing machine. This comes with many pros and cons. One pro is that it improves efficiency such as call response time and lowers service cost.
